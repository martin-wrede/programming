1 3D Virtual Cards

These 3D templates are a concept that is not only suitable for greeting cards, but also for the design of company websites and e-commerce shops. Here you can insert temporary content such as Christmas, summer season and other holidays.
The 3D 'React-Three-Fiber' (3D animation) was a tutorial by Paul Henschel, one of the contributors of the open source library.
Here I have added some additional functionalities, such as the form-text interaction with the 3D letters.

Link: martin-wrede.github.io/numbers9-text/
  • React JS
  • React-Three-Fiber
